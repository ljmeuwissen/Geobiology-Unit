Lesson 3: WHAT WAS EARLY EARTH LIKE?

So far this semester we have been studying geology and Earth's lithosphere. But the lithosphere doesn't just exist by itself. It exists along with the other spheres of the Earth: the Atmosphere, the Hydrosphere, and the Biosphere. When we studied the Water Cycle, we learned about some of the ways that the hydrosphere and atmosphere interact with the lithosphere. Now, we will learn about how the biosphere interacts with the lithosphere. Geobiology is about how life affects geology and how geology affects life.
